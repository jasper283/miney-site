Miney is a minimalist expense tracker that helps you manage your money with less effort. It keeps income, expenses, transfers, budgets, accounts, and reports in one clean place, so you can understand your finances without dealing with unnecessary complexity.

## Features

### Quick bookkeeping

Record income, expenses, and transfers in seconds. Miney is designed to make everyday tracking fast, lightweight, and easy to keep up with.

### Multiple accounts

Manage all of your accounts in one place and get a clear summary of your overall balance and cash flow.

### Personalized categories

Create custom categories that match the way you actually spend, earn, save, and plan.

### Visual reports

Use line charts and pie charts to review your spending patterns and understand where your money goes.

### Flexible budgets

Set budgets that fit your goals and stay aware of your spending before it goes over your target.

### Bill search

Find and summarize transactions quickly with keyword search and filters.

### Multi-device sync

Keep your financial data up to date across devices, so your accounts stay in sync wherever you are.
